I'M YANGHEE ESTHER KIM

My name is Yanghee Esther Kim and I am currently studying at University of California Irvine to learn in-depth in UI/UX and looking for a career opportunity in the design industry.

As a designer, by simplifying the complex processes for the users is at my utmost goal. I am passionate about creating innovative solutions to show the users a thoughtful and meaningful experiences to elevate their experience.

One of the simple reasons why I love design is through everyday learning. By learning something new everyday, I can optimize, improve and have a more effective process. By constantly being curious about how and why things are made the way they are for what purpose is the questions that I ask myself during design process.
